Why we exist

Everyday digital tasks — compressing an image before an upload, merging a few PDFs, checking a word count, converting currency, generating a QR code — shouldn't require an account, a subscription, or sending your files to a stranger's server. ToolGalaxy.online exists to make these small but frequent jobs instant, private, and free.

Every tool on the Site is built to run directly in your browser wherever technically possible, so your files are processed on your own device rather than uploaded to us. That's not a marketing line — it's the actual architecture behind the tools.
